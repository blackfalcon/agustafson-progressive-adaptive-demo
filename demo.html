Accounting

The accounting program at Nichols College prepares students for a broad spectrum of accounting-focused careers. Accountants play a significant role in most organizations and accounting majors are among the most sought after graduates in today’s workforce.

As an accounting major at Nichols College you will learn the principles, methods, and techniques of accounting. In addition to learning these fundamentals you will also learn how they apply to a variety of accounting careers. Graduates are prepared for accounting opportunities across the entire industry including positions in financial accounting, management accounting, auditing, and taxation. In addition to their major coursework, accounting students at Nichols develop skills in communications, quantitative analysis, and environmental dynamics. Our students emerge as well-rounded graduates and gain a distinct edge as they enter the job market.

A variety of professional opportunities exist in public, private and non-profit settings. Some students choose to prepare for a career as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), while others choose to enter the industry directly upon graduation. Nichols accounting graduates are currently employed in a variety of fulfilling positions at such well-regarded organizations as Ernst & Young, Price Waterhouse/Coopers, Mohegan Sun Casinos, Fidelity Investments, State Street Bank, Sovereign Bank, The IRS, The FBI, and the U.S. Secret Service.

Division III Athletics

Field Hockey

Sixth-year Head Coach Kristan Mallet has guided the Nichols College field hockey team to six straight Commonwealth Coast Conference playoff appearances, including the 2010 CCC title and first NCAA appearance in program history. The NCAA game marked the first on campus for any Nichols team, and paved the way for Nichols College to host the 2011 NCAA Division III Field Hockey Championship.

Bison field hockey student-athletes have consistently brought in numerous post-season accolades, including All-CCC, All-ECAC, All-Region honors, as well as national recognition as an NFHCA All-Academic squad.

The team plays its entire home schedule on Vendetti Field, a state of the art Field Turf surface with Musco lighting.
